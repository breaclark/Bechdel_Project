#Data Dictionary

year - the year the movie came out

imdb - the imdb id required to look the movie up in the imdb database

title - the title of the movie

clean_test -  why the movie fails the bechdel test or if it passes

binary - if the movie passes or fails the bechdel test

genre - the genre of the movie

age_rating - the age rating of the movie (R, PG, etc)

budget_2013 - the budget of the movie, adjusted to the year 2013

star_rating - the star rating of the movie on imdb

cast - the cast average, the calculation of which is described in the paper

writer - the cast average, the calculation of which is described in the paper
